Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-12

What we collect

Rotection collects only what is needed to provide the service:

  • Account data: username, hashed password, selected roles, and division choices.
  • Scan data: flagged Roblox users, Discord IDs, and scan history.
  • Audit logs for security-related actions (admin approvals, scan actions).
  • Roblox OAuth data (if you choose to verify): Roblox user ID, username, and OAuth tokens.

Roblox OAuth

Division Leaders can optionally verify via Roblox OAuth. We do not accept or store .ROBLOSECURITY cookies. OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest and used only to verify group membership and perform group moderation actions you explicitly trigger.

How we use your data

Your data is used to authenticate you, run scans, show results, and enforce access control. Scan results are used to help moderators identify flagged users.

Data retention

Scan data is retained up to the configured scan history limit. You can disconnect Roblox OAuth at any time, which removes stored tokens.

Third-party services

We call Roblox and Rotector APIs to fetch group and flag data. Those services have their own policies.

Contact

If you have privacy questions or want your account removed, contact the admin for this deployment.
